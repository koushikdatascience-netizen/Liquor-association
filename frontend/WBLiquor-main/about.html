Since 2003

A voice for licence
holders in West Bengal.

A registered society working for the welfare, representation and modernisation of Foreign Liquor and IML licence holders across the state.

Mission

To safeguard the collective interests of licence holders, advocate fair policy and elevate professional standards across the trade.

Vision

A modern, transparent and digitally enabled association that empowers every member with the tools and voice they deserve.

Objectives

  • Represent licence holders before regulatory bodies
  • Provide legal, regulatory and compliance guidance
  • Circulate policy updates and notifications promptly
  • Facilitate a modern digital membership experience
  • Promote welfare programmes for members and staff
